About

A trip to Ooty with friends is a refreshing escape into nature's beauty. Nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, Ooty offers cool, crisp air and lush greenery. The journey itself, with winding roads and stunning views, sets the stage for adventure. Days are spent exploring tea gardens, boating on Ooty Lake, and trekking through verdant landscapes. The charming streets, vibrant markets, and delicious South Indian cuisine add cultural richness. Evenings around a bonfire, under a starlit sky, with laughter and conversations, make the Ooty trip an unforgettable experience of friendship.
